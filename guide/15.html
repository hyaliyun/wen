Species interactions: Changes in migration patterns and breeding behaviors can also affect the interactions between different species. For example, if a predator species adjusts its migration schedule in response to warmer temperatures, this could have cascading effects on its prey species, which may need to adjust their own behaviors to avoid predation. Overall, the increase in temperature can have complex and far-reaching effects on animal migration patterns and breeding behaviors.
